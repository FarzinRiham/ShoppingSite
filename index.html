~Find the right style for you~

It's time to research! Below we have a few categories of different fashion styles that we believe can apply to you! Read these to get a better feel about which style better suits your taste so you can hone down better on your shopping experience!

Goth is a very popular style in fashion culture and embraces the dark meaning in it's styles. Most Gothic clothing use a variety of solid darker colors, with some of the more popular colors being red, purple, black, orange, or even a dark jade green. Gothic style doesn't necessarily apply to religion as it is stereotyped to be so, and can be worn by anybody who feels that this is their pursuit! You may want to look for dresses, corsets, skirts, and shoes that fall within this category. It also helps to add custom stitching options such as lace or jewel embroidery to finish the touch of your gothic outfit!

Coquette styles are almost like the opposite of gothic trends (that is, if you exclude dark coquette). A lot of coquette culture embraces the color more than the shape or the design of the outfit material, and ranges in colors specifically around pink and white (sometimes even orange and yellow if your looking for a more sunny vibe). Coquette has a lot of sub styles within it that can also vary in the vibe of your outfit. You can range from wearing cute cottage core style dresses and bowtied ribbon shoes to pink and white falred pants with a simple shirt to go with it. The possibilities are endless with this style, and you should totally go for it if you feel it fits you!
